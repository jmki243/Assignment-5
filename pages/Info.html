Info

Located in Freeside just outside the Strip, the Atomic Wrangler is a one-stop-shop for every pleasure one could desire outside of New Vegas itself.

The Atomic Wrangler opened in May 2273 and is located in the heart of Freeside. Since then, it has offered a variety of indulgences - including every type of Hot Pocket you could ever want - to any patrons who find themselves within its inclusive halls.

The Atomic Wrangler offers drinks and entertainment for those looking to relax, and a variety of games including slot machines, card games, and Age of Empires 1 for the more competitive members of our community. The Wrangler also accommodates those looking for - or needing - a place to stay for the night, and caters to any and all forms of the more intimate desires one could have, highlighted by our one-of-a-kind "Gushers and a ball pit" experience.
